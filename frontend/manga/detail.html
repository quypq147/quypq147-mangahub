Tóm tắt

Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit. With his crew of pirates, named the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy explores the Grand Line in search of the world's ultimate treasure known as "One Piece" in order to become the next King of the Pirates. As they journey through uncharted territories and face powerful enemies, the crew discovers the true meaning of friendship and courage.
